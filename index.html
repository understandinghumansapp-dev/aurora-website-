Designed for neurodivergent minds

Built for the way

Aurora was designed from the ground up for ADHD, autism, dyslexia, and other ND profiles. No complex menus, no setup wizards, no cognitive overload — just a conversation. She meets you where you are, every time.

  • Plain English only — no syntax or commands to memorise
  • No context switching — your entire workspace in one window
  • Aurora remembers where you left off, every session
  • One step at a time — say "keep going" and she handles the rest
  • No decision fatigue — Aurora proposes, you approve
  • Forgiving input — she corrects typos and interprets intent
  • Works at your pace — no timeouts, no pressure
  • Dark interface by default, easy on the eyes
